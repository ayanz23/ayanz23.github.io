Built with: Python
Yoga Pose Detector is a sophisticated computer program that uses advanced pose estimation libraries to analyze yoga poses. By leveraging OpenPose and MediaPipe, the application can detect human body keypoints with high precision, enabling accurate pose identification The application processes live video input to detect and map body joint landmarks, calculate joint angles and body alignment, and classify yoga pose variations. Development centered on creating an accessible tool that democratizes high-quality yoga instruction, making guidance available to anyone with a camera and passion for wellness.
